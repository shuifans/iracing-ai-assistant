## Laser Scanning Technology

iRacing pioneered the use of laser scanning technology in sim racing. Using LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging), iRacing captures track surfaces with millimeter precision. This means:

- **Every bump and surface irregularity** is accurately reproduced
- **Kerb heights and track limits** are true to real life
- **Elevation changes** are precise, not approximated
- **Track evolution** is captured through periodic re-scans
